========================================
HOW TO ADD YOUR LOGO AS FAVICON
========================================

Your logo is perfect for a favicon!

========================================
SETUP STEPS:
========================================

1. Save your logo image in the app/ folder

Rename it to exactly: icon.png

Location:
app/icon.png

========================================
NEXT.JS AUTO-DETECTION:
========================================

Next.js 14 automatically uses:
- app/icon.png as the favicon
- Shows in browser tabs
- Works for all devices

No code changes needed!

========================================
ALTERNATIVE: Use SVG
========================================

If you have SVG version:

1. Save as: app/icon.svg
2. Or: app/icon.png

Next.js will automatically use it!

========================================
SIZES NEEDED:
========================================

For best results:
- PNG: 512x512 pixels (square)
- SVG: Any size (scalable)
- Format: PNG or SVG

Your logo should work great!

========================================
VERIFY IT WORKS:
========================================

After adding icon.png:
1. Build the app
2. Check browser tab
3. You'll see your sailboat logo!
