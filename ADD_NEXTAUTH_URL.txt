But NEXTAUTH_URL is missing ❌

========================================
DO THIS IN RENDER DASHBOARD:
========================================

1. Go to Render Dashboard
2. Click your service: lovewaving
3. Click "Environment" tab
4. Click "Add Environment Variable"
5. Enter:
   Key: NEXTAUTH_URL
   Value: https://lovewaving.onrender.com
6. Click "Save Changes"
7. Service will auto-restart

========================================
VERIFY:
========================================

After restart:
1. Go to /health page
2. Click "Debug Info" button
3. Should see: "hasNextAuthUrl": true

Then you can test signup!
